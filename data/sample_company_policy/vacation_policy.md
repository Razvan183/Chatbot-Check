# Vacation Policy

## Annual Allowance

Full-time employees receive 18 paid vacation days per calendar year during
their first two full years of employment.

After completing two full years of employment, full-time employees receive 21
paid vacation days per calendar year. Vacation allowance is prorated for
employees who join during the year.

## Requesting Vacation

Vacation requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 10 business
days before the planned absence. The employee's direct manager must approve the
request before travel or other commitments are booked.

Requests for more than 10 consecutive working days also require approval from
the department director.

## Carryover and Expiration

Employees may carry over a maximum of 5 unused vacation days into the next
calendar year. Carried-over days must be used by March 31 or they expire.

Unused vacation days above the 5-day carryover limit are not paid out unless
local employment law requires otherwise.

## Changes and Emergencies

Employees should update or cancel approved leave in the HR portal as soon as
their plans change. For an unexpected personal emergency, employees should
contact their manager directly instead of waiting for the normal notice period.
