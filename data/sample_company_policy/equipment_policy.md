# Company Equipment Policy

## Issued Equipment

Eligible employees receive a company laptop and standard accessories required
for their role. Equipment remains the property of Northstar Labs and must be
used primarily for authorized work.

Additional equipment costing up to €200 requires manager approval. Requests
above €200 also require approval from the IT Operations manager.

## Care and Acceptable Use

Employees must take reasonable care of company equipment and must not allow
friends or family members to use it. Devices must not be left unattended in a
vehicle overnight.

Employees may not disable device-management, antivirus, encryption, or
automatic security updates installed by IT.

## Loss, Theft, or Damage

Lost, stolen, or seriously damaged equipment must be reported to both the IT
Help Desk and the employee's manager within 24 hours of discovery.

If a device may contain company data, the employee must also report the event
to the Security team immediately so access can be revoked or the device can be
remotely wiped.

Employees should provide the asset tag, last known location, and a short
description of what happened. Suspected theft should be reported to local
authorities when safe.

## Return of Equipment

All company equipment must be returned on or before the employee's final
working day. IT will provide return instructions and, when necessary, a prepaid
shipping label.
