# Sick Leave Policy

## Reporting an Absence

Employees who are unable to work because of illness must notify their direct
manager no later than 09:30 on the first day of absence. The notification may
be sent by phone, chat, or email.

Employees are not required to share a diagnosis with their manager. They
should provide an estimated return date when possible.

## Medical Documentation

Sick leave lasting 3 calendar days or fewer does not require a medical
certificate.

An absence lasting longer than 3 consecutive calendar days requires a medical
certificate. The certificate must be sent confidentially to HR within 2
business days after the employee returns to work.

## Returning to Work

Employees should tell their manager when they are fit to return. If temporary
work adjustments are medically recommended, the employee should discuss them
with HR rather than sending medical details to the wider team.

## Caring for a Family Member

An employee who needs time away to care for an ill immediate family member
should contact HR. HR will explain which leave category applies based on the
employee's location and circumstances.
