# Remote Work Policy

## Regular Remote Work

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week when their role is eligible.
The remaining working days must normally be spent at the employee's assigned
office.

The employee and direct manager must agree on the regular remote-work schedule.
Managers may require office attendance for customer meetings, training, or
team events with at least 2 business days' notice.

## Working From Another Location

Employees may temporarily work from another location within their country of
employment for up to 20 working days per calendar year. They must notify their
manager before the temporary arrangement begins.

Working remotely from another country is not allowed without written approval
from both HR and the Legal team. Requests must be submitted at least 30 calendar
days in advance because tax, immigration, and data-protection rules may apply.

## Availability and Workspace

Remote employees must be reachable during their team's core collaboration
hours, from 10:00 to 16:00 in the employee's local time zone.

Employees are responsible for maintaining a private, professional workspace
and a stable internet connection. Confidential conversations must not be held
where unauthorized people can hear them.

## Security

Only company-managed devices may be used to access confidential company
systems. Employees must use the company VPN on public or shared networks and
must immediately report suspected security incidents according to the Security
Policy.
